About Me:

I am Rohit Kundu (he/him), a senior undergraduate at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Jadavpur University, India. I will be joining the Video Computing Group headed by Prof. Amit K. Roy-Chowdhury in Fall 2022 to pursue my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering.


I work on Deep Learning mainly for Computer Vision applications, with a recent focus on reducing supervision in vision tasks. I primarily code in Python and use the PyTorch, Keras, and TensorFlow libraries for deep learning and have published several papers in reputed peer-reviewed journals and conferences (Google Scholar). I am also a reviewer for notable IEEE, Elsevier, Springer and Wiley journals (Publons Profile).

I have had the opportunity to work in the following institutions:

University of Surrey, UK

Kyungpook National University, South Korea

University of California, Riverside, USA

École de technologie supérieure Montréal, Canada

Jadavpur University, India
